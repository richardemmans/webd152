Activities at Pacific Trails Resort

Hiking

Explore miles of scenic coastal trails with breathtaking ocean views. Guided hikes are available for guests of all skill levels.

Kayaking

Paddle along the Pacific shoreline and discover marine wildlife. Equipment rentals and instruction are available daily.

Bird Watching

Observe native birds and migrating species in their natural habitats. Bring your binoculars and enjoy peaceful mornings outdoors.
